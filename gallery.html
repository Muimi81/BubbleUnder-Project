Below are some of the great views that club members have captured on film (or digital memory), on various dive trips.

This turtle was spotted swiming around the Great Barrier Reef (Queensland, Australia) quite gracefully, despite having had a large chunk taken out of its right side, presumably in a shark attack. [Photographer: Nzangi Muimi]

Jill was just snorkelling when she took this picture - the jellyfish was only a couple of feet under the surface, hence the light is excellent. Jill assures us that the jellyfish had no "tasty, stingy, dangly bits"! [Photographer: Jill Smith]

"I was right next to him as he bit chunks of coral off for dinner - what a sound!" So describes club member Paul who took this shot on Fiji.
